How to Make Fast Withdrawals

How can I speed up my withdrawal process?

To ensure the fastest withdrawal processing, follow these steps: Complete your KYC verification first, ensure all bonus wagering requirements are met, limit yourself to maximum 3 withdrawal requests at a time, and use the same payment method you used for deposits.

What is KYC and why do I need to complete it?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process required by law. You'll need to provide a government-issued ID and proof of address. Completing KYC before requesting withdrawals eliminates delays and ensures instant processing once approved.

Why should I use the same method for deposits and withdrawals?

Using the same payment method for both deposits and withdrawals is a security requirement and speeds up processing. It helps verify account ownership and reduces the need for additional security checks.

What happens if I have active bonus wagering requirements?

Withdrawals are only processed after all bonus wagering requirements are completed. If you have an active bonus, you must meet the wagering requirements first, or forfeit the bonus to proceed with your withdrawal.

Why is there a limit of 3 withdrawal requests?

The 3 withdrawal request limit helps streamline processing and prevents system overload. Once a withdrawal is processed, you can submit new requests. This ensures faster processing times for all players.
